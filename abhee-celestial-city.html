When it comes to real estate investment in Bangalore, the golden rule has always been to follow the infrastructure. As we look towards 2026 and 2026, no micro-market presents a more compelling case than the Varthur–Sarjapur Road corridor in East Bangalore.

1. The Infrastructure Boom: PRR and Metro Phase 3

The upcoming Peripheral Ring Road (PRR) is set to revolutionize connectivity in East Bangalore. By seamlessly connecting Tumkur Road, Bellary Road, Old Madras Road, and Hosur Road, the PRR will drastically cut down commute times. The proposed Metro Phase 3 extensions will further integrate this corridor — a classic catalyst for exponential property appreciation.

2. GCC Expansions and The IT Triangle

Varthur and Sarjapur sit at the apex of Bangalore's most lucrative IT triangle: Whitefield, Outer Ring Road (ORR), and Electronic City. With GCCs aggressively expanding along the ORR-Sarjapur stretch, historical data shows a consistent, double-digit CAGR for premium real estate in this belt.

3. Why Pre-Launch is the Best Entry Window
