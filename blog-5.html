2BHK vs 3BHK: The Ultimate Dilemma

1. Budget and Maintenance

The most immediate factor is your budget. A 2BHK is naturally more affordable upfront and comes with lower maintenance costs. However, with flexible financing options available today, upgrading to a 3BHK might only marginally increase your monthly EMI while offering significantly more value.

2. Space and Future-Proofing

Post-pandemic, the need for an extra room has skyrocketed. Whether it's for a home office, a guest room, or a growing family, a 3BHK future-proofs your investment. If you plan to stay in the apartment for over 10 years, a 3BHK is highly recommended.

3. Resale Value and Rental Demand

While 2BHKs are easily rented out to young couples and bachelors, 3BHKs have a higher resale demand among established families looking to upgrade. In premium corridors like Sarjapur, 3BHKs hold their value exceptionally well.

5. FAQs

Which configuration gives better rental yield?

Percentage-wise, 2BHKs often provide a slightly higher rental yield, but 3BHKs attract longer-term, stable family tenants.
