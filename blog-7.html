The Benefits of Gated Townships

Published on April 22, 2026 | By Lifestyle & Design Team

1. Uncompromised Security

The primary draw of a gated township is security. With 24/7 CCTV surveillance, manned entry points, and smart visitor management systems, residents enjoy absolute peace of mind. Children can play freely, and elderly family members can take their evening walks safely.

2. A World of Amenities

Townships are micro-cities. They offer clubhouses, Olympic-sized swimming pools, extensive sports courts, convenience stores, and even polyclinics inside the premises. You don't have to navigate city traffic for your daily recreation or essential needs.

3. Community and Networking

Living in a massive township fosters a strong sense of community. Festival celebrations, sports tournaments, and cultural events organized within the society allow you to network and socialize with like-minded individuals.

4. Experience Abhee Townships

Abhee Ventures is revolutionizing township living in Bangalore. Our upcoming 45-acre mega-project, Abhee Codename New Dimension in Whitefield, brings Scottish-themed luxury, vast open greens, and world-class sporting facilities to your doorstep.

5. FAQs

Are maintenance charges very high in townships?

Because the costs are shared among hundreds of residents, the per-square-foot maintenance cost is highly competitive and offers incredible value for the amenities provided.

Do Abhee townships have power backup?

Yes, all our projects feature 100% power backup for common areas and adequate backup for individual apartments.
