WHY ABHEE

The Pillars of Our Success

🏛️

100% RERA Approved

Every Abhee project is registered with Karnataka RERA before any marketing or bookings begin. This ensures homebuyers have full legal protection, transparent pricing, and guaranteed construction timelines. Abhee has zero legal disputes and zero encumbrances across its entire project portfolio — a standard upheld since 2009 and never compromised.

💎

Uncompromising Quality

Abhee partners exclusively with Grade-A construction contractors and sources premium materials from certified vendors. Each project undergoes rigorous quality audits at foundation, superstructure, finishing, and handover stages. The result is a consistent 4.5-star rating from homeowners — one of the highest in Bangalore's residential real estate segment for a developer of this size and scale.

📍

Prime Growth Locations

Every Abhee project site is selected based on rigorous infrastructure analysis, employment proximity data, and 10-year capital appreciation modelling. Our micro-market selection process has consistently delivered 15–22% above Bangalore's average capital appreciation, making Abhee projects not just exceptional homes, but exceptional financial investments for every buyer who has chosen to trust us.

🤝

On-Time Delivery

Abhee has delivered every single completed project within its RERA-committed possession timeline. In a market where over 60% of projects face delays, this distinction has earned Abhee a loyal community of repeat buyers and investor referrals. Our construction management team operates 24/7 monitoring protocols to ensure milestone adherence — because we believe your timeline is as valuable as your investment.

🏦

Bank Approved & Loan Ready

All Abhee projects hold pre-approved home loan agreements with India's leading banks including SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis Bank, and Kotak Mahindra. This eliminates financing hurdles for buyers and ensures faster loan disbursement at the most competitive interest rates available. Our dedicated home loan assistance team guides every buyer through the application process at no extra cost.

🌿

Sustainable Living

Abhee integrates sustainability into every project design — from rainwater harvesting systems and solar-powered common areas to EV charging bays and landscaped open spaces that exceed BBMP green coverage norms. Our flagship New Dimension township dedicates 85% of its 45-acre site to open spaces, forests, and water bodies, setting a new standard for eco-conscious luxury living in Bangalore.

Our Story

Building Bangalore's Future, One Landmark at a Time

Founded in 2009 by R. Nagaraj Reddy, Abhee Pvt. Ltd. began with a singular vision — to deliver residences that combine architectural excellence with absolute legal integrity. Over 15 years, that vision has produced more than 16 RERA-approved residential projects and placed over 5,000 families into homes they are proud to call their own.

What sets Abhee apart in Bangalore's crowded real estate market is an unwavering commitment to on-time delivery, RERA compliance, and construction quality. Every project undergoes rigorous quality checks at every stage — from foundation design to final handover inspection. Our construction team partners exclusively with Grade-A contractors and sources premium materials from certified vendors.

Whether it is a 2 BHK apartment on Sarjapur Road, a sprawling 4.5 BHK luxury unit in our flagship New Dimension township, or a plotted villa on Soukya Road — Abhee has consistently delivered landmark living experiences that appreciate in value and lifestyle, year on year.

Prime Corridors

Bangalore's Fastest-Growing Real Estate Corridors

Abhee carefully selects project locations at the intersection of infrastructure growth and lifestyle demand. Here is why each corridor we operate in is a sound investment.

🗺️ Sarjapur & Varthur Road

The epicentre of Bangalore's tech boom. Home to Wipro SEZ, Outer Ring Road IT parks, and upcoming Peripheral Ring Road. Sarjapur Road apartments have delivered a consistent 12–18% YoY appreciation, driven by GCC expansion and Metro Phase 3 plans. Abhee New Dimension is strategically positioned at the heart of this corridor.

🗺️ Whitefield & ITPL

India's Silicon Valley heartland. With the Purple Metro Line now operational through Whitefield, property demand has surged 30% in 2024 alone. Abhee's projects near Whitefield offer seamless access to ITPL, EPIP Zone, and Cessna Business Park — making them ideal for Bengaluru's IT professional community.

🗺️ Electronic City & Bommasandra

South Bangalore's industrial powerhouse, home to Infosys, HP, and Siemens. The Bommasandra Metro extension has unlocked new affordability in the corridor. Abhee Silicon Shine offers well-planned 1 to 4 BHK apartments at competitive price points — a perfect first-home or rental investment in a rapidly maturing micro-market.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Abhee.

Who is Abhee? +

Abhee Pvt. Ltd. is a premier real estate developer headquartered in HSR Layout, Bangalore. Founded in 2009 by Chairman & Managing Director R. Nagaraj Reddy, the company has delivered 16+ residential projects and housed thousands of families across Bangalore's prime corridors.

Are all Abhee projects RERA approved? +

Yes. Every Abhee project is RERA approved and additionally sanctioned by statutory authorities including BMRDA, BDA, and BBMP, ensuring full legal compliance and buyer protection.

Where are Abhee projects located in Bangalore? +

Abhee operates across Bangalore's highest-growth real estate corridors — Sarjapur Road, Whitefield–Varthur Road, Gunjur, Bommasandra, Soukya Road, Kudlu Gate, and Chandapura.

What types of properties does Abhee offer? +

We offer luxury apartments (2 BHK to 4.5 BHK), villa plots, and large-format gated township developments. Projects range from boutique residences to 45-acre integrated townships.

How many projects has Abhee completed? +

Abhee has successfully delivered 16+ residential projects since 2009, including Abhee Kingscourt, Abhee Daffodils, Abhee Nakshatra, Abhee Nandika, Abhee Sunrise, and Abhee Prakruti Villa.

What are the ongoing projects by Abhee right now? +

Current ongoing projects include Abhee Codename New Dimension (Whitefield), Abhee Aaria (Gunjur), Abhee Celestial City (Sarjapur Road), Abhee Silicon Shine, Abhee Natura, Abhee Serenity Springs, and Abhee Riviera Royale.

What is Abhee Codename New Dimension? +

Abhee Codename New Dimension is a flagship 45-acre township project on Whitefield–Varthur Road, Bangalore. It features Scottish-inspired architecture, premium amenities, smart layouts, and excellent connectivity to Outer Ring Road and the IT corridor.

How can I schedule a site visit to an Abhee project? +

You can call us at 098868 90004, email info@abheeprelaunch.com, or fill out the enquiry form on this page. Our team will arrange a personalised site visit at your convenience, Monday to Saturday, 9AM–7PM.

Does Abhee offer home loan assistance? +

Yes. Abhee works with leading banks and NBFCs to assist buyers with home loan processing, documentation support, and pre-approved loan facilities at competitive rates.

Is Abhee the same as Abhee Builders or Abhee Developers? +

Yes — Abhee Pvt. Ltd. is the parent company, also known informally as Abhee Builders or Abhee Developers. All projects are developed and delivered under the Abhee brand umbrella.
