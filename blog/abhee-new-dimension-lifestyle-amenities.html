Abhee New Dimension: Unveiling 140+ Lifestyle Amenities in Bangalore's Grandest Township

1. Introduction to a New Era of Luxury

When we talk about redefining luxury in Bangalore, Abhee New Dimension stands out as the ultimate benchmark. Spanning a monumental 45 acres on the highly coveted Varthur Sarjapur Road corridor, this ambitious project brings the majestic charm of Scottish architecture to India's Silicon Valley. In this blog, we explore the unparalleled suite of 140+ amenities designed to cater to every facet of modern living.

2. 4 Grand Clubhouses: A Masterpiece of Leisure

The centerpiece of community life at Abhee New Dimension is its network of four grand clubhouses, sprawling over an astonishing 1.5 lakh square feet combined. These Scottish-themed structures, with their grand arches and stone facades, are more than just visual marvels. They house world-class facilities including:

  • A fully-equipped gymnasium with crossfit and yoga zones.
  • Multiple temperature-controlled swimming pools.
  • Mini-theaters and banquets for extravagant gatherings.
  • Co-working spaces tailored for the modern remote professional.

3. 3-Acre Golf Driving Range & Sports

A rare offering in Bangalore's real estate, the 3-acre golf driving range provides residents with a premium sporting experience right at their doorstep. For fitness enthusiasts, the township offers an exhaustive list of sports amenities:

  • International standard tennis, badminton, and squash courts.
  • A dedicated cycling and jogging track winding through the lush landscape.
  • Multi-purpose turf grounds for football and cricket.

4. Nature & Wellness: 85% Open Green Spaces

Understanding the need for a serene sanctuary amidst urban chaos, Abhee New Dimension dedicates 85% of its land to open spaces. The architectural vision brings Scottish design elements to life with meticulously manicured gardens, a sprawling man-made loch (lake), and tranquil wellness centers. The township promotes a holistic lifestyle where every resident can reconnect with nature.

5. Why Invest in Abhee New Dimension?

From an investment perspective, securing a unit at the pre-launch stage presents a compelling opportunity for substantial capital appreciation. Combining a unique thematic concept, massive scale, and a trusted developer in Abhee Pvt. Ltd., the project creates a strong value proposition.
