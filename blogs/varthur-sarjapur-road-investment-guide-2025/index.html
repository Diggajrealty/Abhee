Why Varthur–Sarjapur Road Is Bangalore's #1 Investment Corridor in 2025–2026

Published on May 11, 2026 | By The Abhee Editorial Team

Executive Summary

When it comes to real estate investment in Bangalore, the golden rule has always been to follow the infrastructure. As we look towards 2025 and 2026, no micro-market presents a more compelling case than the Varthur–Sarjapur Road corridor in East Bangalore.

1. The Infrastructure Boom: PRR and Metro Phase 3

The upcoming Peripheral Ring Road (PRR) is set to revolutionize connectivity in East Bangalore. By seamlessly connecting Tumkur Road, Bellary Road, Old Madras Road, and Hosur Road, the PRR will drastically cut down commute times. For residents of properties on Varthur–Sarjapur Road, this means effortless access to the airport and other major tech hubs. Additionally, the proposed Metro Phase 3 extensions will further integrate this corridor into Bangalore's rapid transit network, a classic catalyst for exponential property appreciation.

2. GCC Expansions and The IT Triangle

Varthur and Sarjapur sit at the apex of Bangalore's most lucrative IT triangle: Whitefield, Outer Ring Road (ORR), and Electronic City. With Global Capability Centers (GCCs) aggressively expanding their footprint along the ORR-Sarjapur stretch, the influx of highly paid tech professionals is driving unprecedented demand for luxury housing. This sustained demand is why historical data shows a consistent, double-digit CAGR for premium real estate in this belt.
