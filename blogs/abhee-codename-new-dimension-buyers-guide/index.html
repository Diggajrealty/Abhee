Published on May 11, 2026 | By The Abhee Editorial Team

Executive Summary

Are you considering investing in East Bangalore's most anticipated luxury project? Here is your complete, unbiased buyer's guide to Abhee Codename New Dimension, analyzing everything from pricing and RERA compliance to honest pros and cons.

1. Project Overview and RERA Status

Developed by the trusted Abhee, Codename New Dimension is a 45-acre mega township designed around an authentic Scottish theme. For buyers concerned with legality and transparency, you can rest easy: the developer is strictly compliant, and the Abhee New Dimension RERA registration number is PRM/KA/RERA/1251/308/PR/260226/008489.

2. Floor Plans and Pricing

The project caters to diverse needs with meticulously designed floor plans:

  • 2 BHK: (1,180 - 1,250 sq. ft.) Starting from ₹1.14 Cr*
  • 2.5 & 3 BHK: (1,350 - 1,800 sq. ft.) Ranging from ₹1.30 Cr* to ₹1.59 Cr*
  • 3.5 & 4.5 BHK Luxury: (1,950 - 2,600 sq. ft.) Ranging from ₹1.88 Cr* to ₹2.31 Cr*

*(Note: Prices are indicative at ₹9,650–₹11,000/sq. ft. during the pre-launch phase.)*

3. Honest Pros & Cons

Pros: The scale is unmatched. With 85% open space, a 3-acre golf range, a man-made loch, and 4 massive clubhouses, the lifestyle offering is spectacular. Its location adjacent to Greenwood High International School makes it a top-tier choice for families.

Cons: Because it is a massive 45-acre phased development, early residents might experience ongoing construction in later phases. Additionally, the sheer size of the township means residents will rely on internal buggies to traverse from their towers to the farthest amenities.
