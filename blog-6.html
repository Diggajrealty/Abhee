1. Checking Your Eligibility

Your home loan eligibility primarily depends on your age, income, existing liabilities, and credit score (CIBIL). A credit score of 750 or above generally secures the best interest rates from top banks.

2. Essential Documentation

Keep your KYC documents, last 6 months' salary slips, 2 years' Form 16, and bank statements ready. For self-employed individuals, IT returns and business proof are mandatory.

3. Fixed vs Floating Interest Rates

Floating rates are linked to the RBI's repo rate and can change, usually offering a lower initial rate. Fixed rates remain constant, offering predictability. Most home buyers opt for floating rates as they historically prove cheaper over a 15-20 year tenure.

What is the typical down payment required?

Banks typically finance up to 80-85% of the property value. You will need to arrange 15-20% as a down payment.
