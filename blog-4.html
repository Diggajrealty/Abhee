1. What is RERA?

The Real Estate (Regulation and Development) Act, known as RERA, was introduced to protect home purchasers and boost real estate investments. It mandates transparency, timely delivery, and accountability from developers.

2. Benefits for Homebuyers

Investing in a RERA-approved project ensures that your funds are utilized correctly. Developers must keep a significant portion of project funds in an escrow account, ensuring money isn't diverted. It also guarantees compensation for delayed handovers and provides a clear grievance redressal mechanism.

3. Abhee's 100% Compliance

At Abhee Ventures, trust is our foundation. Every single one of our 16+ projects is fully RERA approved. We believe in complete transparency, providing buyers with clear timelines, legal documentation, and peace of mind.

4. FAQs

How do I verify a RERA registration?

You can visit the official Karnataka RERA website and search using the project's unique RERA registration number provided by the builder.

Are all Abhee properties RERA registered?

Yes, absolutely. 100% of Abhee properties are RERA approved, ensuring safety for your investment.
