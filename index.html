Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Abhee Ventures.

Who is Abhee Ventures? +

Abhee Ventures Pvt. Ltd. is a premier real estate developer headquartered in HSR Layout, Bangalore. Founded in 2009 by Chairman & Managing Director R. Nagaraj Reddy, the company has delivered 16+ residential projects and housed thousands of families across Bangalore's prime corridors.

Are all Abhee projects RERA approved? +

Yes. Every Abhee Ventures project is RERA approved and additionally sanctioned by statutory authorities including BMRDA, BDA, and BBMP, ensuring full legal compliance and buyer protection.

Where are Abhee projects located in Bangalore? +

Abhee Ventures operates across Bangalore's highest-growth real estate corridors — Sarjapur Road, Whitefield–Varthur Road, Gunjur, Bommasandra, Soukya Road, Kudlu Gate, and Chandapura.

What types of properties does Abhee Ventures offer? +

We offer luxury apartments (2 BHK to 4.5 BHK), villa plots, and large-format gated township developments. Projects range from boutique residences to 45-acre integrated townships.

How many projects has Abhee Ventures completed? +

Abhee Ventures has successfully delivered 16+ residential projects since 2009, including Abhee Kingscourt, Abhee Daffodils, Abhee Nakshatra, Abhee Nandika, Abhee Sunrise, and Abhee Prakruti Villa.

What are the ongoing projects by Abhee right now? +

Current ongoing projects include Abhee Codename New Dimension (Whitefield), Abhee Aaria (Gunjur), Abhee Celestial City (Sarjapur Road), Abhee Silicon Shine, Abhee Natura, Abhee Serenity Springs, and Abhee Riviera Royale.

What is Abhee Codename New Dimension? +

Abhee Codename New Dimension is a flagship 45-acre township project on Whitefield–Varthur Road, Bangalore. It features Scottish-inspired architecture, premium amenities, smart layouts, and excellent connectivity to Outer Ring Road and the IT corridor.

How can I schedule a site visit to an Abhee project? +

You can call us at 080 4588 8783, email info@abheeprelaunch.com, or fill out the enquiry form on this page. Our team will arrange a personalised site visit at your convenience, Monday to Saturday, 9AM–7PM.

Does Abhee Ventures offer home loan assistance? +

Yes. Abhee Ventures works with leading banks and NBFCs to assist buyers with home loan processing, documentation support, and pre-approved loan facilities at competitive rates.

Is Abhee Ventures the same as Abhee Builders or Abhee Developers? +

Yes — Abhee Ventures Pvt. Ltd. is the parent company, also known informally as Abhee Builders or Abhee Developers. All projects are developed and delivered under the Abhee Ventures brand umbrella.
